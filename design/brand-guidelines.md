## Voice & Tone

### Voice Characteristics
- **Technical but accessible**: Explain complex concepts clearly
- **Confident but humble**: Show expertise without arrogance
- **Direct but friendly**: Get to the point, maintain warmth
- **Professional but human**: Enterprise-quality with personality

### Writing Guidelines
- Use active voice: "Detect issues" not "Issues are detected"
- Be concise: Remove unnecessary words
- Use concrete examples: Show don't tell
- Avoid jargon: Explain technical terms when needed
- Write for developers: Assume technical knowledge
