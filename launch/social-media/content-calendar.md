## Content Mix Guidelines

### Daily (Twitter)
- 2-3 tweets/day
- Mix: features (40%), engagement (30%), results (20%), fun (10%)
- Always respond to mentions/replies

### 3x/week (LinkedIn)
- Long-form posts (thought leadership)
- Professional tone, data-driven
- Cross-link to blog/docs

### 3x/week (Instagram)
- Visual-first, text secondary
- Stories: behind-the-scenes, polls
- Reels: product demos, tips

### 2x/week (Short Video)
- TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts
- <30 seconds, hook in first 3s
- Trending audio when relevant

### 1x/week (Long Form)
- Dev.to article
- YouTube video
- Reddit deep dive
- Hacker News discussion
