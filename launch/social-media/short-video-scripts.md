## Production Tips for Short Videos

### Filming
- Use phone (vertical mode locked)
- Natural light or ring light
- Clear audio (wired earbuds mic works)
- 60fps for smooth motion

### Editing
- CapCut (free, easy)
- Fast cuts (attention span <3s)
- Text overlays (80% watch muted)
- Trending audio increases reach

### Captions
- Auto-captions mandatory (accessibility)
- Large font (readable on small screens)
- High contrast (white text, dark background)

### Posting Strategy
- Best times: 7-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 7-9 PM
- Use 3-5 hashtags (mix popular + niche)
- Reply to comments within 1 hour
- Cross-post to TikTok, Reels, YouTube Shorts
