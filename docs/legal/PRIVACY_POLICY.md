# Privacy Policy for ODAVL Studio

**Last Updated: December 3, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to ODAVL Studio ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

**ODAVL Studio** is a platform for autonomous code quality consisting of three products:
- **ODAVL Insight**: ML-powered error detection
- **ODAVL Autopilot**: Self-healing code infrastructure
- **ODAVL Guardian**: Pre-deploy testing and monitoring

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address, name, password (hashed)
- **Workspace Data**: Code repositories, project configurations, workspace settings
- **Usage Data**: Error signatures, code analysis results, recipe trust scores
- **Payment Information**: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store credit card details)

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Log Data**: IP address, browser type, operating system, access times
- **Usage Analytics**: Feature usage, performance metrics, error logs
- **Cookies**: Session cookies, authentication tokens (see Cookie Policy)

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- **GitHub OAuth**: Profile information (username, email, avatar) if you sign in via GitHub
- **Google OAuth**: Profile information (email, name, avatar) if you sign in via Google

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

### 3.1 Service Delivery
- Provide and maintain ODAVL Studio services
- Analyze code for errors and security vulnerabilities
- Generate improvement recommendations
- Execute self-healing recipes (with your consent)
- Monitor application quality and performance

### 3.2 Account Management
- Create and manage your account
- Authenticate your identity
- Process payments and subscriptions
- Send service-related notifications

### 3.3 Improvement and Development
- Improve ML models (error detection, trust prediction)
- Develop new features and detectors
- Analyze usage patterns (anonymized)
- Conduct research on code quality

### 3.4 Communication
- Send product updates and announcements
- Provide customer support
- Send marketing communications (with your consent - you can opt out anytime)

### 3.5 Legal and Security
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect against fraud and abuse
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Resolve disputes

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We **do not sell your personal data**. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

### 4.1 Service Providers
- **Hosting**: Vercel (Next.js hosting), AWS/Azure (cloud infrastructure)
- **Database**: PostgreSQL (Supabase or similar)
- **Analytics**: PostHog, Mixpanel (anonymized usage data)
- **Payment Processing**: Stripe (PCI-DSS compliant)
- **Error Monitoring**: Sentry (error logs)

All service providers are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

### 4.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or legal process, or to:
- Protect the rights, property, or safety of ODAVL Studio, our users, or the public
- Respond to government requests
- Enforce our Terms of Service

### 4.3 Business Transfers
If ODAVL Studio is acquired or merged with another company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your data is transferred.

### 4.4 With Your Consent
We may share your information for any other purpose with your explicit consent.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while your account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: Data deleted after 2 years of inactivity (with 30-day warning)
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data permanently deleted within 90 days of account deletion
- **Legal Requirements**: Data retained longer if required by law

**Exception**: Anonymized, aggregated data (e.g., ML training datasets) may be retained indefinitely for research purposes.

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, and Other Privacy Laws)

Depending on your location, you have the following rights:

### 6.1 Right to Access
Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you

### 6.2 Right to Rectification

### 6.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

## 7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption**: Data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Authentication**: Secure password hashing (bcrypt), OAuth 2.0
- **Access Control**: Role-based access, least privilege principle
- **Monitoring**: Real-time security monitoring, intrusion detection
- **Audits**: Regular security audits and penetration testing
- **Incident Response**: Data breach notification within 72 hours (as required by GDPR)

**Note**: No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
