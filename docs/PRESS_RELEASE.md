## Three Specialized Products

**ODAVL Insight** uses machine learning to detect errors across 16+ categories, including security vulnerabilities, performance issues, and code complexity problems. Unlike traditional static analysis tools, Insight prioritizes issues based on historical data and project-specific patterns, reducing noise by up to 80%.

**ODAVL Autopilot** goes beyond detection by autonomously fixing issues through its proprietary O-D-A-V-L cycle: Observe, Decide, Act, Verify, and Learn. Every change is cryptographically attested for compliance and audit trails, with built-in rollback capabilities for safety.

**ODAVL Guardian** validates deployments before they reach production, testing accessibility, performance, security, and SEO. With visual regression testing and multi-browser validation, Guardian catches issues that traditional CI/CD pipelines miss.

## Autonomous, Not Automatic

Unlike fully automated tools that make changes without oversight, ODAVL maintains human control while automating grunt work. Every change is reversible, verified against quality metrics, and requires human approval before merging.

"We call it 'autonomous' because the system makes intelligent decisions, but humans remain in control," explained [Founder Name]. "Think of it as hiring a junior engineer to do the repetitive work, with a senior engineer reviewing — except the junior engineer never gets tired and learns from every mistake."

## Safety-First Design

ODAVL includes three layers of safety:

1. **Risk Budget Guards**: Maximum 10 files per automated cycle, with protected paths that require manual review
2. **Undo Snapshots**: Every change is stored with full rollback capability
3. **Attestation Chain**: Cryptographic proof of every improvement for SOC 2 and compliance audits

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How is ODAVL different from SonarQube or Snyk?**  
A: SonarQube and Snyk focus on detection. ODAVL detects, fixes autonomously, and validates before deploy. We cover security, performance, complexity, and more in one platform.

**Q: Is autonomous code fixing safe?**  
A: Yes. We have three layers of safety: risk budget guards (max 10 files per cycle), undo snapshots (reversible changes), and attestation chains (audit trails). Every change requires human approval before merging.
