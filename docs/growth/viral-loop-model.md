# ODAVL Viral Loop Model

## K-Factor Calculation

**Formula**: K = i × c

Where:
- **i** = Average invites sent per user
- **c** = Conversion rate (invites → signups)

### Target Metrics

| Metric | Target | Current |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Invites per user | 5 | TBD |
| Conversion rate | 20% | TBD |
| **K-Factor** | **1.0+** | TBD |

### Viral Coefficient

- **K < 1**: Sub-viral (paid growth needed)
- **K = 1**: Self-sustaining growth
- **K > 1**: Viral growth (exponential)

## Growth Mechanics

### 1. Invite Incentives

**User Benefits**:
- 3 invites → 1 month Pro free
- 10 invites → 3 months Pro free
- 25 invites → 1 year Pro free

**Friend Benefits**:
- Referred users get 2 weeks Pro trial (vs 1 week standard)

### 2. Social Sharing

**One-Click Share**:
- Pre-filled Twitter/LinkedIn posts
- Custom referral URLs with UTM tracking
- Share buttons on dashboard, pricing, docs

### 3. Product-Led Growth

**Built-in Virality**:
- Team invites in onboarding flow
- Share results with teammates
- Public project badges (GitHub README)

## Optimization Strategy

### Phase 1: Measure (Month 1)
- Track K-factor weekly
- A/B test incentive tiers
- Analyze drop-off points

### Phase 2: Optimize (Month 2-3)
- Increase invite prompts
- Improve conversion messaging
- Add gamification (leaderboards)

### Phase 3: Scale (Month 4+)
- Automate rewards fulfillment
- Launch ambassador program
- Partner with influencers

## Success Criteria

**Month 1**: K-factor > 0.5 (baseline)  
**Month 3**: K-factor > 0.8 (sub-viral)  
**Month 6**: K-factor > 1.2 (viral)
