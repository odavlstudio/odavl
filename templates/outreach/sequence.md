# ODAVL Outreach Sequence - Day-by-Day Action Plan

## Overview

**Objective**: Book 20 pilot demos (10 DE + 10 Gulf) leading to 6-8 pilot conversions
**Timeline**: 3-week sequence with systematic follow-ups
**Success Metrics**: 15%+ reply rate, 30%+ demo conversion, 50%+ pilot conversion

## Day 0: Initial Outreach

### Morning Preparation (9:00-10:00 AM)

- [ ] Review `companies.csv` for today's targets
- [ ] Personalize email templates with company-specific research
- [ ] Verify all contact information and LinkedIn profiles
- [ ] Prepare tracking spreadsheet for responses

### German Market Outreach (10:00-12:00 PM)

**Target**: 10 companies from DE market
**Template**: `templates/outreach/email_en.md` (Initial outreach)
**Personalization Focus**:

- Reference TypeScript/React job postings
- Mention GDPR compliance capabilities
- Include European data sovereignty points

### Gulf Market Outreach (2:00-4:00 PM)  

**Target**: 10 companies from Gulf market
**Template**: `templates/outreach/email_ar.md` (Initial outreach)
**Personalization Focus**:

- Reference regional tech hubs (Dubai, Riyadh, Doha)
- Mention Arabic language support
- Include references to digital transformation initiatives

### Day 0 Deliverables

- [ ] 20 personalized emails sent
- [ ] Tracking sheet updated with send times
- [ ] Calendar blocks reserved for potential demos
- [ ] LinkedIn connection requests sent (where appropriate)

## Day 1-2: Response Management

### Daily Response Processing

- [ ] Check email responses hourly during business hours
- [ ] Reply to interest within 2 hours maximum
- [ ] Schedule demos immediately using calendar link
- [ ] Update tracking sheet with response status

### Demo Scheduling Process

1. **Immediate Reply**: Thank for interest, confirm demo
2. **Calendar Link**: Send direct booking link with 3 time options
3. **Demo Prep**: Add to CRM, prepare company-specific demo notes
4. **Confirmation**: Send calendar invite with demo materials 24h before

## Day 3: First Follow-up Wave

### German Market Follow-up (10:00-11:30 AM)

**Template**: `templates/outreach/email_en.md` (Follow-up #1)
**Target**: Non-responders from Day 0 DE outreach
**Angle**: Focus on specific pain point question

### Gulf Market Follow-up (2:00-3:30 PM)

**Template**: `templates/outreach/email_ar.md` (Follow-up #1)  
**Target**: Non-responders from Day 0 Gulf outreach
**Angle**: Focus on specific pain point question

### Day 3 Actions

- [ ] Send follow-up emails to all non-responders
- [ ] Update tracking with follow-up timestamps
- [ ] Conduct any scheduled demos from Day 0-2 responses
- [ ] Follow up on demo attendees within 24 hours

## Day 4-6: Demo Execution & Pilot Conversion

### Demo Week Schedule

**Target**: 6-8 demos total from initial outreach
**Duration**: 15 minutes each (strict timing)
**Script**: `sales/DEMO_90S.md` adapted for live presentation

### Post-Demo Process (Within 24 Hours)

1. **Thank You Email**: Recap key points discussed
2. **Pilot Proposal**: Detailed 14-day pilot plan
3. **Technical Setup**: Calendar setup call if interested
4. **Follow-up Schedule**: 3-day, 1-week follow-up sequence

### Demo-to-Pilot Conversion Tracking

- [ ] Demo completion rate (target: 80% show rate)
- [ ] Pilot interest rate (target: 50% express interest)  
- [ ] Setup call conversion (target: 70% of interested)
- [ ] Actual pilot start rate (target: 50% of demos)

## Day 7: Second Follow-up Wave

### Template Used

**English**: `templates/outreach/email_en.md` (Follow-up #2)
**Arabic**: `templates/outreach/email_ar.md` (Follow-up #2)

### Case Study Approach

- [ ] Customize case study to match prospect's industry/size
- [ ] Include specific metrics relevant to their challenges
- [ ] Maintain urgency without being pushy
- [ ] Provide easy scheduling option

### Day 7 Metrics Check

- [ ] Total response rate achieved (target: 15%+)
- [ ] Demos scheduled from sequence (target: 6-8)
- [ ] Pilots initiated (target: 3-4 by this point)
- [ ] Pipeline value calculation

## Day 8-13: Pilot Support & Management

### Active Pilot Management

**Pilots Running**: 3-4 expected active pilots
**Daily Check-ins**: 5-minute status calls
**Issue Resolution**: <2 hour response time for pilot issues
**Success Metrics Tracking**: ESLint warnings, dev team feedback

### Pilot Success Criteria

- [ ] 40%+ ESLint warning reduction by Week 1
- [ ] Zero production incidents during pilot
- [ ] Positive developer feedback on UX
- [ ] Clear ROI demonstration

### New Lead Nurturing

- [ ] Continue following up on remaining prospects
- [ ] Share pilot success stories (anonymized)
- [ ] Invite to observe pilot results calls
- [ ] Maintain touchpoints with interested non-starters

## Day 14: Final Follow-up Wave

### Last Outreach Attempt

**Template**: Follow-up #3 (Final attempt)
**Approach**: Respectful closing with future opportunity mention
**Timing**: Coordinated to avoid pilot participants

### Extended Pilot Offers

- [ ] 30-day extended pilots for high-value prospects
- [ ] Dedicated success manager assignment
- [ ] Priority support and customization
- [ ] Q1 follow-up scheduling for non-starters

## Success Metrics & KPIs

### Email Performance Targets

- **Send Rate**: 20 emails (100% of target)
- **Open Rate**: 40%+ average
- **Reply Rate**: 15%+ overall
- **Demo Conversion**: 30%+ of replies

### Pipeline Targets

- **Demos Scheduled**: 6-8 from 20 outreach
- **Pilots Started**: 3-4 from demos
- **Commercial Conversions**: 2-3 from pilots
- **Revenue Pipeline**: $50k+ ARR potential

### Quality Metrics

- **Response Time**: <2 hours to inbound interest
- **Demo Show Rate**: 80%+ attendance
- **Pilot Completion**: 90%+ completion rate
- **NPS Score**: 8+ average from pilot participants

## Contingency Plans

### Low Response Rate (<10%)

- [ ] Revise subject lines and email copy
- [ ] Increase personalization depth
- [ ] Try different contact channels (LinkedIn, phone)
- [ ] Review ideal customer profile fit

### Demo No-Shows (>30%)

- [ ] Improve confirmation sequence
- [ ] Offer more flexible scheduling
- [ ] Provide demo recording option
- [ ] Follow up with rescheduling offer

### Pilot Technical Issues

- [ ] Escalate to technical team immediately
- [ ] Provide daily status updates
- [ ] Offer alternative solutions
- [ ] Document lessons learned for process improvement

## Tools & Resources Required

### Outreach Tools

- **Email Platform**: Gmail/Outlook with templates
- **Calendar**: Calendly or similar booking system
- **CRM**: Basic tracking in CSV/spreadsheet
- **LinkedIn**: Sales Navigator (recommended)

### Demo Materials

- **Script**: `sales/DEMO_90S.md`
- **Recording**: Live screen share preferred
- **Backup**: Pre-recorded demo if technical issues
- **Materials**: Follow-up emails with resources

### Success Measurement

- **Tracking Sheet**: Response rates, demo outcomes, pilot status
- **KPI Dashboard**: Real-time metrics monitoring
- **Pipeline Reports**: Weekly progress summaries
- **Feedback Collection**: Post-demo and post-pilot surveys

**Note**: This sequence is designed for manual execution. Automation tools can be added later but human personalization and relationship-building are critical for enterprise prospects.
