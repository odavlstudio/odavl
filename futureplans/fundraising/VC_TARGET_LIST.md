## Outreach Strategy

### Week 23: Warm Intro Requests (30 intros)

**Day 1-2: Identify mutual connections**
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator search
- Email introduction service (Intro.co, Warm introductions)
- Founder network (YC, Techstars alumni)

**Day 3-5: Request intros (10/day)**
- Email template: "Quick favor - intro to [VC name]"
- Provide: One-liner, deck, why this VC specifically
- Follow up in 48 hours if no response

**Sample Intro Request:**
```
Subject: Quick intro to [Partner Name] at [Firm]?

Hi [Mutual Connection],

Hope you're doing well! Quick ask:

I'm raising $2M for ODAVL (AI that fixes 78% of code issues automatically). We have 50 beta users, $10K MRR, 4.8/5 satisfaction.

I'd love an intro to [Partner Name] at [Firm] because:
- [Portfolio company X] synergy (similar GTM/tech)
- [Firm thesis Y] aligns with our roadmap
- [Specific reason Z]

Would you be open to making an introduction? Happy to provide more context or jump on a quick call.

Deck: [link]
Demo: [link]

Thanks!
[Name]
```

### Week 23-24: Cold Email Outreach (20 emails)

**Target:** VCs without warm intro path

**Best practices:**
- Subject: "ODAVL: AI that fixes 78% of code issues" (specific number = curiosity)

## Meeting Scripts

### First Call (30 min)

**Agenda:**
1. Intro (2 min) - Who we are, why we built this
2. Problem (3 min) - 20-30% time waste, $18B market
3. Solution (5 min) - Demo of 78% auto-fix
4. Traction (5 min) - 50 users, $10K MRR, 4.8/5 rating
5. Vision (3 min) - $100M+ ARR roadmap
6. Ask (2 min) - $2M seed, $10M post-money
7. Q&A (10 min)

**Key Talking Points:**
- "78% auto-fix rate - no competitor has this" (core differentiator)
- "GitHub has 100M developers - built-in distribution" (GTM advantage)
- "18x LTV/CAC - capital efficient growth" (unit economics)
- "Patent-pending O-D-A-V-L cycle" (defensibility)

**Objection Handling:**
- "Market too crowded" → "SonarQube/ESLint have 0% auto-fix, we're the only solution"
- "Too early" → "50 beta users validated 78% fix rate, ready for GTM"
- "Team too small" → "$1M hiring budget, experienced founders"
- "Engineering risk" → "Already at 92.3% ML accuracy, proven tech"

**Close:**
"If you're interested, next step is partner meeting with full deck. Can we schedule for next week?"

### Partner Meeting (60 min)

**Agenda:**
1. Full pitch deck (15 min) - Walk through all 15 slides
2. Product deep-dive (15 min) - Live demo, architecture Q&A
3. Market deep-dive (10 min) - TAM, SAM, SOM, competition
4. Unit economics (10 min) - Walk through financial model
5. Q&A (10 min)

**Bring:**
- Laptop with demo (offline version in case of wifi issues)
- Printed 1-pager (leave-behind)
- Reference customer contacts (if requested)

**Close:**
"What are next steps? Do you need any additional information for due diligence?"
