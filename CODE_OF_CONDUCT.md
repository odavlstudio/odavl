# ODAVL Code of Conduct

ODAVL is committed to a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful community.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and considerate
- No harassment, discrimination, or abusive language
- Respect differing viewpoints

## Enforcement

Violations may result in removal from the project. Report issues to the maintainers at <support@odavl.com>.
