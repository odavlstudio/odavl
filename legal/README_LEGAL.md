## Legal Review Requirements

⚠️ **IMPORTANT**: All documents in this directory are templates and require professional legal review before production deployment.

### Required Legal Actions
1. **Jurisdiction Selection**: Specify governing law and court jurisdiction
2. **Company Information**: Add complete company address and registration details
3. **Contact Details**: Verify all email addresses and phone numbers are operational
4. **Regulatory Compliance**: Ensure compliance with local and international laws
5. **Insurance Coverage**: Verify liability coverage matches limitation clauses

### Customization Requirements
- Enterprise customers may require contract-specific modifications
- International customers may need jurisdiction-specific terms
- Industry verticals (healthcare, finance) may require additional compliance clauses
- Data residency requirements may affect DPA terms
